Frequently Asked Questions

.NET Conference 2025 FAQ

How do I register for the .NET Conference?
Registration is simple – just visit our website, fill in your details, and choose your pass. Early bird discounts apply!
What topics will be covered at the conference?
Our sessions cover a wide range of topics including ASP.NET Core, modern C#, microservices, cloud integration, DevOps, and more.
Are there any prerequisites for attending sessions?
No prior experience is necessary. Our sessions are designed for both beginners and seasoned developers.
Can I attend individual sessions without a full pass?
Full conference passes are required to access all sessions, workshops, and networking events.
Will the sessions be recorded?
Yes, all sessions will be recorded and available for registered attendees after the event.
What is the refund policy?
Please refer to our registration page for detailed refund and cancellation policies.
Is there a code of conduct?
Yes, all attendees must adhere to our code of conduct to ensure a respectful and inclusive environment.
How can I become a sponsor?
For sponsorship inquiries, please contact our team via the contact section below.
